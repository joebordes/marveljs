DatatablesVery mature. Has everything you will need, but it is jquery based.
SmartTablesThis is the one if we go AngularJS. If we bring in the responsiveness of Tablesaw and some of the features of Datatables, we should have everything we need.

Second Round

After the first round I set the scores on the different grids which led me to only evaluate two in the second round:

Smart Table

I couldn't get sort nor search to work, so I didn't insist. Not being responsive out of the box will make me go for the other tool.

trNgGrid

The second round here was a breeze. Much like the first round which made a good impression I simply went through the different features carefully and they all worked except selecting rows, even paging against a 32000 record database. This is the one.
